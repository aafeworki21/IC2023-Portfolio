LEGOs!

From a young age, I had an incredible sense of creativity

and an imagination that I manifested into what I built from

LEGOs. I was that one kid in first grade building mechs while

everyone else was building houses. It was through LEGO that

I was able to explore my innovative skills at a young age and

enhance my problem solving capabilities. I decided after

falling in love with building things I would persue a career

in doing so.
